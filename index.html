3Times eSports

3Times is a very large project, going back around a few years back when gaming and esports sector reached its maximum splendor with the so-called and well-known game "Fortnite".

At the beginning of 2021, a group of friends who were dedicated to competing seriously in national tournaments and "LAN" made the decision to create a team that would be recognized on a large scale among the Hispanic and English community, obviously and like everything else, the team He made himself known among the different social networks: "Instagram", "Twitter", "TikTok", "Facebook" and "Twitch".

During the whole week working day and night without any rest, they were planning and creating the logos, banners, videos, trailers, social networks, publications and most importantly. Find the players and management team that would carry this great project forward.

Finally, after all the work carried out in such a short time, it was decided to present and start the "3T" project. It was a complete success, we got more than 100,000 visits, 14,000 followers and 6,000 likes in less than 24 hours on "Instagram". The creators of the project were very enthusiastic about the great community that we could reach and we wanted to go ahead and give 100% of our effort.

Currently, 3Times is recognized as a possible vacancy to access the franchise league of several of the most important Competitive Games in history.
