WHO WE ARE?

We are 3 Times eSports, a competitive team that emerged in the 21st century in the middle of 2021, we are a team that emerged from the dreams of many people to be the best players in the world, and to belong to one of the best franchises in the present.

WHICH IS OUR OBJECTIVE?

Our goal is to become the best community and esports team that has been able to make itself known in this long time that the sector has been making itself known.

We plan to do many collaborations with different companies that are currently focused on gaming, sports and other related options, we also want to bring our audience experiences that have never been presented to the wide community of followers that we have, both events and face-to-face tournaments. , raffles more things.

We will also give the best player who is showing us that he wants and deserves to belong to a place in our team the opportunity to join our rooster of players, this will not be easy since you will compete against yourselves and other players to win this great opportunity that we are presenting, having said that, thank you very much and good luck!
